General Updates

First Aid

2016-04-10

Yesterday, we held a First Aid course for our Waterkeepers and Fishing Guides at The Munster Arms hotel.
A huge thanks goes out to club member, John O' Brien who was our instructor for the course. John, who is also a Paramedic really brought home to us, the importance of having basic First Aid. We would encourage anyone and everyone to do the same. You could save a life.

8lb Springer

2016-04-06

More good news as a club member reported catching an 8lb springer yesterday evening on the fly. Water is perfect at the moment, with showers keeping the water at a steady level. Forecast is cold with showers of rain, which may be heavy on Sunday. With the great tides though and fish present, it is well worth a cast. Attached is a photo of a nice brown trout I recently released whilst salmon fishing. Great to see this calibre of trout present.

Congratulations Anthony Buckley

2016-04-05

Congratulations to committee member Anthony Buckley for catching this fine 12lb springer today whilst spinning. Water is perfect for fishing at the moment, hopefully we don't get too much rain as a couple more fish have also been seen.

Water Level: 0.17m
Water Temp: 10.8 degrees Celsius.

River Update 31/03/16

2016-03-31

We've enjoyed glorious sunshine but endured icy showers the last few days in West Cork. The Bandon rose last Sunday and we were hoping a few salmon may be caught. However, just one sea liced fish has been reported on the river since the weekend. Rain is forecast the next couple of days and tides are building up early next week so prospects are good for the beginning of April.

Water Level: 0.05m.
Water Temp: 9 degrees Celsius.

River Update

2016-03-21

The river has dropped to a low level, with the banks and pools getting a much needed rest after all the winter floods. An angler met one springer up river during the week but no more fish were reported landed since last Saturday's hat trick. A few fish have been seen the last couple of days but the easterly winds and bright sunshine aren't helping.

Water Level: -0.02m.
Water Temp: 9.4 degrees Celsius.
